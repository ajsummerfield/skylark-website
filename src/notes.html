Home page

I've made the video full screen, but a common problem is that when switching to a mobile view, the text is clipped (I'm assuming you're wanting to use the MOVING_IMAGES.mp4 video).

Typically, the text is not in the video background, but overlayed ontop as to keep the correct dimensions and feel in different device views.

It also clashes somewhat with core messaging eg, 'Welcome to skylark'. I'd suggest (for this case), of using a video without any text

So, I've added a dummy video, and a few options. If you want to continue with the real video as the text, if you could supply the video without text and an indication of layout (as per home-3), that would be great.

Also, the navigation itself doesn't have the greatest contrast and the background colours aren't the best. An white version of the logo would help as you would normally put a darker backgorund overlay ontop of a video with lighter text ontop of that

With logo, the general trend is that they or more horizontally focused on the web, otherwise it makes some of the text a little harder to read or makes the navigation bars too large
