Who We Are

Skylark International is a relational network of Churches, Charities and Ministries

Why Skylark International?

However well a church or organisation is functioning, we believe there is always greater possibility and greater power when we connect and collaborate with others.

Skylark International is a family, providing a place of belonging, connection and equipping for churches, networks, charities and individuals.

Held together by relationship and united around shared vision and values, we’re not a corporate, cookie-cutter style network but an organic, diverse and multi-faceted expression of the kingdom.
